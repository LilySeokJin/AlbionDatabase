Mongol Khanate

Mongol Khanate which is a primarily EU, Lymhurst based guild focused on both PvP and PvE. We offer daily activities such as Fame Farming, Ganking, Hellgates, Mage Raids, Avalonian Raids, Crystal League, and are involved in regular ZvZ Content! We are new player friendly, Territory owners with Hideouts in The Roads of Avalon and Blackzone. We are looking for newer players with 5m+ Total Fame who are willing to learn from some of our more veteran players, and dip their toes into end-game content. We are also looking for Veteran players who want to take part in End-Game Content and regular ZvZ fights
